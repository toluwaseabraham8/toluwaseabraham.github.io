About

Strategy that starts inside the brand.

I'm a brand and content strategist based in Ibadan, Nigeria with hands-on experience building content ecosystems for ministry organisations, nonprofits, and education brands. My work spans social media strategy, brand identity, CMS governance, SEO content, and crisis communications — always built around systems that scale.

I believe a brand story cannot stick if there are no internal believers. That principle drives every engagement: I build inward first, then outward — culture before content, structure before publish.

I bring additional skills in photography, videography, copywriting, and public relations that make me a full-stack creative partner, not just a strategist on a deck.

Strategic Objectives

  • Brand awareness: 50% of city families reached within 12 months
  • 5,000 engaged cross-platform followers by month 18
  • 10+ high-impact thought leadership pieces within 24 months
  • 500 annual class/teacher inquiries by year two
  • 10+ local organisation partnerships

Target Audiences

  • Parents of children ages 4–18 (middle to upper-middle class)
  • Music teachers and instructors ages 22–45
  • Community leaders and music enthusiasts
  • Local schools seeking extracurricular programs

Five Content Pillars

  • Child Development Through Music — cognitive skills, emotional intelligence, social growth
  • Celebrating All Music — classical to contemporary, genre spotlights, student performances
  • Empowering Teachers — instructor spotlights, teaching tips, career growth
  • Community & City Pride — local history, partnerships, cultural events
  • Thought Leadership — trends, research, innovations in music pedagogy

Content Types

  • Blog posts, videos, social media, email newsletters
  • Whitepapers, infographics, podcasts/webinars
  • Press releases, influencer collaborations

6-Month Rollout Plan

  • Months 1–2 (Brand Introduction): Launch #MusicForAll campaign, open house, welcome newsletter — target 1,000 website visits, 500 followers
  • Months 3–4 (Community Engagement): Child development content, local festival performance, "Share Your Music Story" campaign — target 2,000 followers, 50 inquiries
  • Months 5–6 (Thought Leadership): Whitepaper launch, panel discussion, #MusicEdTips series — target 3,000 followers, 200 inquiries, 2 media guest posts

Channel Strategy

  • Website (central hub), Instagram (visual), Facebook (community)
  • YouTube (long-form), TikTok (creative shorts), LinkedIn (teacher recruitment)
  • X Platform (real-time), Email (segmented), Local media (PR)
